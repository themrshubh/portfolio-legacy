Android Application for Crop Disease Detection
Mental disorders are one of the leading causes of illnesses worldwide. Of them, depression is a common mental disorder, and the World Health Organization has classified it as one of the main reasons of disability worldwide. Recently, the number of mental illnesses has increased exponentially. Consequently, the burden on mental health practitioners has risen. The worsening ratio of mental health workers to patients has caused a substantial increase in doctors' workload. To help mental health practitioners cope with this increasing workload, we propose a system that assists doctors to monitor patients' responses and progress. Monitoring a patient's response to treatment plays a vital role in depression management. While monitoring is necessary, it is a very cumbersome and tedious task, being quite time-consuming. The proposed system uses Alexa -- a voice-based virtual assistant to monitor the patient's response to treatment between sessions scheduled by the psychiatrist.
